From all dark for hundreds of days to suddenly significant change color to orange, even light yellow, the east coast part of NSW where the city Sydeney sits has been suffering from Covid-19 a lot.

You could try to hover over a community to view it's important census data and Covid-19 case number's disturibution on the box plot. Hover over a box plot's axis to view stastic details. Click on the hide text button on the right to hide this part of text to view the box plot and the map at the same time.

The communities that have the most number of Covid-19 cases is postcode 2170. With 27545 as the number of Covid-19 cases, it is a huge outlier that even can not be displayed on the box plot. The reason behind that huge amount of Covid-19 cases can be observed on the box plot, as it's population is also a huge outlier. With a very high population and a relative small area, this communities has a higher than average Covid-19 case by population rate and must be paid attention to if there are more infection waves to come.

The situation for community that has a postcode of 2145 is almost the same as 2170. Both of them are huge outliers in the aspect of Covid-19 cases and population. Noticibaly, they all have lower than average median ages. This might be a cause for the large amount of infection since younger group are more likely to gather together and tends to live in a more populated area. Both of them have a high enemploy rate as well.

The unemployment result doesn't seem to be the same for community 2570. This community has a low unemployment rate but still has a very high Covid-19 case number and Covid-19 case per population. The median age in that area is low, and population is high, which is a pattern that's common for vulnearable communities.

Going through most communities with high Covid-19 case numbers, a potential of low median age can be observed. Community 2444 is an exception. It has a higher than average median age but still has a high Covid-19 case number because of the fact it is highly populated. Although it's case number is high, it's case per population stat is low.

Community 2775 is a special one among all the communities with high Covid-19 case numbers surrounding it. The main reason of this might be it's relative low population and relative high median age.



Pattern

To observe the pattern more accurately, a correlation matrix is calculated by total Covid-19 cases and census data of each community. The higher the absolute value of the correlation, the more we need to pay attention to.

Obiously, population has the highest degree of coordination with Covid-19 cases, with almost fully linear. The line graph shows the correlation during different variants. Click on the circles that are circled by other variable's color to hide those variables on the line graph. The correlation between population and Covid-19 case numbers have an overall potential of increasing and comes to a peak at Omicron variant. This suggests that Omicron is more dangerous to those communities with high population and new variants might have a high effect on highly populated communities.

Population and Covid-19 case number per population though, is different. Click the button on the top of line graph to change to display lines for Covid-19 case per population. The variant that has the lowest correlation for Covid-19 case number and population, Delta, actually have the highest Covid-19 per population / population correlation.

Another interesting relationship is the one between Covid-19 case number and median age*. The effect is that the lower the age, the more the virus cases. It is clear that this effect has been enlarged during the development of the virus.

Although that might be caused by the correlation between median age and population , the relationship between median age and Covid-19 case per population suggests that the average age of a community does have an effect on the number of Covid-19 cases and must be taken care of.

*If the line of the variable is not displayed on the line graph, please click on the highlighted circle on the correlation matrix.


Summary

To conclude, communities with high population and low median age must be taken extra care when a new Covid-19 vaiant outbreaks and even they are not affected much in the past, it's still likely they will suffer from high number of infections. Communities in or near Sydney more need extra protection as well. Communities with high unemployment are slightly more vulnearable to Covid-19 and might need some attention.
